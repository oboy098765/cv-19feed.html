Coronavirus(Covid-19)

Definition

The coronavirus is also popularly known as Covid 19.This virus is a life taking virus which is currently widespread across the world. The coronavirus has cost so many individuals their lives and more are still yet to die.

The coronavirus as we all know is a respiratory disease causing virus. This virus, as the name implies attaches itself to the lungs and multiplies creating clones of itself across so that they affect the system. This usually takes about 7-14 days as it is the incubation period. Then after this period of time, symptoms appear making the human body on the verge of malfunctioning.

But as the popular statement implies, "Corona Virus is not a death sentence."

Prevention is Better than cure.

There is only one known cure for the Coronavirus, so to say, and that is...

Good hygiene

When you hear about the phrase 'Good hygiene', what tends to come to the mind of a regular person is,"Oh! Have you had your bath?" Or even,"Why are your clothes dirty?" Well, this is, in a way,is personal hygiene but the personal hygiene that is currently streaming foreign news is "2020 personal hygiene." This is somehow like an every man for himself situation because you can't trust anyone! But the only things that still seem sane to do in a group are these forms of personal hygiene.

Mantain social distancing

In times like this, people should really look back at the world two years ago and ask themselves,"Why didn't I enjoy the world back then?" Make hay when the sun shines, so they say. Go hide when the moon glows, so I say. There's nothing really much into this social distancing. It's really just staying away from everyone else in the world for a while. No biggie! Since the particles from our mouths move by air it is very important to keep a measurable distance of about 6 feet from everyone else. So that the particles may drop to the ground or surfaces around without touching us.

Wash your hands regularly

Most of us are used to washing our hands with refreshing soap and scrubbing in between our fingers with that scratchy feeling of satisfaction, but all of a sudden it's such a big deal. Well, we should all endeavour to wash our hands with soap and water(preferrably warm water) for at least 20 seconds before deciding "you know what,I think I'm wasting water."

Avoid touching your eyes, nose and mouth when hands are not washed.

Once in a while, we feel the urge to just itch around the nose and the eyes. Even, bite finger nails. But people should really stop because once that is done, you're indirectly saying,"Adios,mama!" and "Halo ha isolation centers." And I know that's not a good experience. Linked to this, do not eat food without washing your hands. Most people are fund of this. I went to a restaurant one day and the table in front of me was already eating. I thought,"Wait a sec..." to my surprise the waiter served the table their appetizers before bringing in the hot towels. And, they fell for it. I was all like, disgusting. So please, if you're going to touch your eyes or other vital parts of the body, please keep your hands squeaky clean. Also food doesn't taste as good as it does when your hands are clean. :)
